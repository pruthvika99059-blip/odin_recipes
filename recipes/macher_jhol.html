Macher Jhol

Description

Macher Jhol is a traditional Bengali fish curry made with fresh fish, potatoes, and a rich, spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg fresh fish, cut into pieces
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and add the cumin and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add the fish pieces and cook until they are golden brown.
  3. Add the turmeric and red chili powder. Mix well.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and bring it to a boil.
  5. Add the potatoes and simmer for about 15 minutes until the potatoes are cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with rice or roti.
