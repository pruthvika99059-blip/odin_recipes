Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a delicious tomato-based sauce infused with butter and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with salt, garam masala, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides.
  3. Add the tomato puree to the pan and cook for about 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors are well combined.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
