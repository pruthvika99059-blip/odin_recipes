Dhokla

Description

Dhokla is a popular Gujarati snack made from fermented chickpea batter, typically served with chutney or pickle.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mix the chickpea flour with water and lemon juice. Let it ferment for 2-3 hours.
  2. Heat a pan and add the cumin and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add the fermented batter and cook until it is set.
  4. Sprinkle the turmeric and red chili powder. Mix well.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or pickle.
